Icon Percentage

Place the percentage inside the attribute "data-value", the active color in "data-color-active" and the inactive color in "data-color-inactive". Inside the canvas tag is a fallback icon in case a canvas is not supported.

 
<canvas class="fawesome fa-percentage big" data-value="65" data-color-active="#00cdff" data-color-inactive="#c7e2ea">
    <div class="fawesome fa-circle big"></div>
</canvas>
 
<canvas class="fawesome fa-percentage" data-value="90" data-color-active="#00cdff" data-color-inactive="#c7e2ea">
    <div class="fawesome fa-circle"></div>
</canvas>
 
<canvas class="fawesome fa-percentage smaller" data-value="30" data-color-active="#00cdff" data-color-inactive="#c7e2ea">
    <div class="fawesome fa-circle smaller"></div>
</canvas>
 
<canvas class="fawesome fa-percentage small" data-value="55" data-color-active="#00cdff" data-color-inactive="#c7e2ea">
    <div class="fawesome fa-circle small"></div>
</canvas>
