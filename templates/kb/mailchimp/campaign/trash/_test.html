Hallo *|FNAME|*,

August is coming to an end with great events such as „775 Jahre Berlin “ and „Tanz im August “ but the upcoming month is equally filled with new highlights: The Berlin Music Week   – 5 to 9 September - is just around the corner, followed by the European Creative Industries Conference at 6 September and the first Berlin Art Week from 11 to 16 September.

Kreatives Brandenburg welcomes its new content partners: startnext , livekritik , the Berlin Music Commision , furthermore we are supporting the campaign „ Musik 2020 Berlin “ for the development of Berlin as a music hub as well as our new media partner Experimentdays 2012 .

What else is happening in the city? This newsletter will keep you informed about new portfolios, call for tenders and culture news from Berlin.

Enjoy!!

Your Kreatives Brandenburg Team

© Katharina Scherer  / pixelio.de

 

Being creative – what is to be done? The Ikea-principle turns political: Today it’s about freedom, tomorrow about surving: self organization as imperative of the hour? ... more...

Berlin as strong IT location represents itself with a new marketing campagne: The new campagne was realized by the Senate Department for Economics, Technology and Research, Berlin Partner, the Chamber of Industry and Commerce of Berlin as well as the regional IKT-Association SIBB e.V. in order to draw attention and arouse curiosity of IT users to information technoloy „made in Berlin“...more...

Berlin Music Week 2012: Experts on Music Supervisor Sessions: The Berlin Music Commision has invited top-class experts such as Angela Leus, Director of Film Music at Universal Pictures, to the Berlin Music Week 2012 . ..more...

Social Networks become more popular in the event management industry: The Social Media Report 2012 by the ticket provider amiando has shown that 84% of the interrogated event agencies use facebook as platform for marketing ...more...

Culture Brand Award 2012: there is still time: Cultural providers and managers, communities and associations from the German-speaking area are invited to apply for the „Kulturmarken-Award 2012“ until 31 August 2012 ...more...

What’s the state of Berlin’S IKT-Economy? Potential analysis of the IHK Berlin: With this survey, the IHK wants to find out more about the situation and the requirements of entrepreneurs on economical and political basic conditions in Berlin. Participation until 9 September 2012 ...more...
